Fruit and Vegetable

Reason for Selection

"Fruit and Vegetable" was chosen as it conveys the freshness and variety of fruits and vegetables that the website offers. It is easy to remember, relevant to the content, and helps build a brand focused on health and organic produce.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Fruit and Vegetable is to serve as an online hub for customers who wish to explore a wide range of fresh fruits and vegetables, get information about their nutritional benefits, and place orders for home delivery. It will also provide seasonal produce updates, recipes, and tips for sustainable consumption.

Scenarios

  • What are the seasonal vegetables available for delivery this month?
  • Can I get organic fruits delivered to my area?
  • What are the nutritional benefits of kale and how can I incorporate it into my diet?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Fresh Green (#4CAF50) – This color will be used for the website's header, navigation bar.
  • Accent Color: Vibrant Orange (#FFA500) – Used for buttons, links, and other call-to-action elements.
  • Background Color: Light Beige (#F5F5DC) – A soft neutral color that will be used for the main page background, making it easy to read text and keeping the focus on content.

Typography

  • Font 1: Poppins (Headings) – This font is modern and clean, ideal for headings and subheadings to make the content stand out.
  • Font 2: Open Sans (Body Text) – A simple and highly readable font for the main body text to ensure that visitors can easily read and digest the content on the page.
  • Font 3: Pacifico (Special Sections or Logo) – A playful and unique font that will be used for the logo or special sections like banners or quotes, giving the site a touch of personality.
